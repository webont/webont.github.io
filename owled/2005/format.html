Workshop Format:

The goal of the workshop will be to maximise discussion. The technical sessions will therefore consist of short presentations of papers (grouped by topic area) followed by directed discussion, and a poster session, also followed by directed discussion.

The workshop may also have one session in common with the International Conference on Rules and Rule Markup Languages for the Semantic Web (RuleML-2005) in which the integration of OWL with rules languages will be discussed.
