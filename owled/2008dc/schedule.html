Scheduling for OWLED 2008 DC
OWL: Experiences and Directions

Here is a tentative schedule for OWLED2008DC. If you think there should be changes to this schedule, please send email to pfps@research.bell-labs.com.

A regular paper is alloted 20 minutes and statements of interest is alloted 10 minutes (both including questions). Demos will be allocated 15 minutes for presentation (including questions) with some time at the end of the demo session for interaction. There will be a data projector (XGA) available for all presentations.
