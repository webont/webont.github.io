[ Submissions | Registration | Papers | Schedule | Dates | Organization | Venue | Contacts ]

The sixth workshop in the The OWL: Experiences and Direction (OWLED) workshop series will again bring users, implementors and researchers together in order to measure the current state of need against the state of the art and to set an agenda for language evolutions that satisfy users. This OWLED workshop will last two days and will be held in the Washington, D.C., area, adjacent to the Eighth International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC 2009), in late October 2009.
